Children’s Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 16, 2026

This Children’s Privacy Policy explains how WordHub handles information in a safe and privacy-focused way for children and families.

1. Intended Use

WordHub is a vocabulary learning application designed for children. It is intended to support language learning through structured practice and repetition in a safe and age-appropriate environment.

2. No Personal Data Collection

We are committed to protecting children’s privacy. This app does not:

  • Require users to create an account
  • Collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal identifiers
  • Collect precise location data
  • Access contacts, photos, or device identifiers

3. Local Data Storage

All learning progress, vocabulary records, and settings are stored locally on the user’s device. We do not store this data on external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not include third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services. No user data is shared with third parties for advertising or profiling purposes.

5. WebView Usage

The app may use embedded WebView components to display informational content, such as policy pages or learning-related content.

When web content is loaded, standard technical data (such as IP address, device information, and browser-related information) may be transmitted as part of normal internet communication. This data is not collected, stored, or used by us for identification, tracking, or analytics.

We do not use WebView for advertising, behavioral tracking, or user profiling.

6. Children’s Privacy

Protecting children’s privacy is a core principle of this app. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children, and the app is designed to be safe for independent or supervised use depending on local regulations.

7. Data Security

All data is stored on the user’s device and is protected by the device’s own security mechanisms (such as passcodes or biometric locks). We do not have access to user data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Children’s Privacy Policy from time to time to improve the app or comply with legal requirements. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: admin@studywiseai.com

儿童隐私政策

生效日期：2026年6月16日

本儿童隐私政策用于说明 WordHub 如何以安全、保护隐私的方式处理儿童及家庭的信息。

1. 使用目的

WordHub 是一款面向儿童的词汇学习应用，通过结构化练习与重复训练，帮助儿童在安全、适龄的环境中进行语言学习。

2. 不收集个人信息

我们高度重视儿童隐私。本应用不会：

  • 要求用户创建账号
  • 收集姓名、邮箱、电话号码等个人信息
  • 收集精确位置信息
  • 访问通讯录、照片或设备唯一标识符

3. 本地数据存储

所有学习进度、词汇记录与设置仅存储在用户设备本地，不会上传至任何外部服务器。

4. 第三方服务

本应用不包含广告、统计分析或用户行为追踪服务，也不会与第三方共享用户数据用于广告或用户画像分析。

5. WebView 使用说明

本应用可能使用内嵌 WebView 用于展示信息类内容，例如隐私政策页面或学习相关内容。

在加载网页内容时，可能因正常网络通信产生技术性信息（如 IP 地址、设备信息、浏览器相关信息）。这些信息不会被我们收集、存储或用于用户识别、追踪或数据分析。

本应用不会通过 WebView 进行广告投放、行为追踪或用户画像分析。

6. 儿童隐私保护

保护儿童隐私是本应用的核心原则。本应用不会主动或故意收集儿童个人信息，设计上确保可安全使用，部分情况下可在家长或监护人指导下使用。

7. 数据安全

所有数据仅存储在用户设备本地，并受设备自身安全机制保护（如密码或面容识别）。我们无法访问用户数据。

8. 政策更新

我们可能会不时更新本儿童隐私政策，以改进产品或满足法律要求。更新内容将发布在本页面。
