Privacy Policy - WordHub·华文词汇

Effective Date: June 16, 2026

1. Overview

WordHub·华文词汇 (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) is a vocabulary learning application designed for children. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting user data.

This App operates fully on-device and does not require user accounts or cloud storage.

2. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal data on external servers.

All learning data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Vocabulary learning progress
  • Practice history
  • App preferences
  • Local study records (SQLite / device storage)

We do NOT collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location
  • Any contact information

3. Data Storage

All data remains on the user’s device. We do not operate any backend server.

4. Third-Party Services

Version 1.0 does NOT use any third-party analytics, advertising, or AI services.

Future versions may introduce optional services such as Firebase Analytics or AI features. If so, this policy will be updated accordingly.

4A. Use of WebView

The App uses embedded web views (WebView) to display certain content, such as informational or policy pages.

When web content is loaded, standard technical information (such as IP address, device information, and browser-related data) may be transmitted as part of normal internet communication. This data is not collected, stored, or used by us for identification, tracking, or analytics purposes.

We do not use WebView for advertising, user tracking, or behavioral profiling.

5. Children’s Privacy

This App is designed for children. We do not collect personal data or show ads.

All learning data and personal progress records are stored locally on the user’s device. We do not collect or store personal data on external servers.

6. Security

Data security depends on the user’s device security (e.g. passcode, Face ID). We do not have access to user data.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy in future versions.
